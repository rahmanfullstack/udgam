About UDGAM

Founded in 2019
We give hope to the hopeless and help to the helpless

UDGAM is a non-profit organization that aspires to change lives by empowering rural people. An equal opportunity to serve free education in a technical base to rural people by some education scheme and institutions. UDGAM Social Education & Welfare trust seeks highly motivated and the qualified individuals to engage with us in areas such as rural transformation, feeding, development and education. Our team contributes to a positive social impact through diverse community engagement initiatives. The trust is currently focusing on great changes in the areas of rural developments, education, and feeding , etc.

Our Mission

We will be the pillar for any sort of social help, social awareness in Burla.

Having a team of all dedicated students, it’s time to revolutionize the young generation and make everyone to reach out all the need of livelihood.

Helping and supporting the people who couldn’t get medical treatment due to being less with finance, transportation and standard of living.

Being the member of “UDGAM”, it’s our prime responsibility to lead the society on a good note and make every facility accessible despite socio-economic conditions.
